Prompt Writing Tips

A few small tweaks make a huge difference in image quality.

Be specific

Instead of "a dog," try "a golden retriever puppy playing in autumn leaves."

Mention lighting

Words like "golden hour," "neon glow," or "soft morning light" shape the mood instantly.

Add a shot type

"Close-up portrait," "wide landscape shot," or "aerial view" guide composition.

Pick an art style

Use the style buttons above, or describe one yourself: "oil painting," "pixel art," "ink sketch."

Layer your details

Subject → setting → mood → style works well: "a fox, in a snowy forest, peaceful, watercolor."

Iterate, don't settle

Didn't love the result? Tweak a few words and regenerate — small changes = different outputs.

How It Works

AI image generation, explained simply

Behind the scenes, ImagineAI sends your words to an AI model trained on millions of image–text pairs. That model has learned the relationships between language and visuals — so when you say "a cat astronaut," it knows what a cat looks like, what a space suit looks like, and how to blend the two.

It then builds the image gradually, starting from random visual "noise" and refining it step by step until it matches your description — a bit like a sculptor slowly revealing a shape from a block of marble.

  • No artistic skill needed — your words are the brush.
  • Every image is generated fresh, just for your prompt.
  • Powered by the Pollinations AI Image Generation API.
